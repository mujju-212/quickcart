## 🔎 Product Search & Filters

### Using the Search Bar

**Location**: Top header, always accessible

#### Basic Search
1. Click search bar
2. Type product name, brand, or keyword
3. Press Enter or click search icon
4. View results page

**Search Examples:**
```
"tomato"          → Finds all tomato products
"dairy"           → Shows dairy category items
"500g"            → Products with 500g size
"milk organic"    → Organic milk products
```

#### Search Features
- **Auto-suggest**: Suggestions appear as you type
- **Recent Searches**: Your last 5 searches saved
- **Popular Searches**: Trending search terms
- **Spell Check**: Suggests corrections for typos

### Filtering Products

**Available Filters:**

#### 1. Category Filter
- Select from dropdown
- Filter by specific category
- "All Categories" to reset

#### 2. Price Range Filter
- Set minimum price

## 🛒 Adding Items to Cart

### Quick Add from Product Card

**Method 1: Single Click Add**
1. Find product on homepage or search
2. Click **"Add to Cart"** button
3. Confirmation toast appears
4. Cart count updates in header

**Default Quantity**: 1 item

### Detailed Add from Product Page

**Method 2: Quantity Selection**
1. Open product details page
2. Adjust quantity using **+/-** buttons
3. Click **"Add to Cart"**
4. Item added with selected quantity

**Quantity Controls:**
```
┌─────────────────┐
│  [-]  [2]  [+]  │
│                 │
│ [Add to Cart]   │
└─────────────────┘
```

- **Minimum**: 1 item
- **Maximum**: Available stock or 10 (whichever is lower)
- **Step**: Increase/decrease by 1

### Adding from Wishlist

1. Go to **Account** → **Wishlist**
2. Click **"Add to Cart"** on wishlist item
3. Item moves to cart with quantity 1

### Re-ordering from Order History

1. Go to **My Orders**
2. Select previous order
3. Click **"Reorder"** button
4. All items added to cart
5. Review and modify quantities
6. Proceed to checkout

### Cart Operations

#### Update Quantity
1. Find item in cart
2. Click **+** to increase
3. Click **-** to decrease
4. Price updates automatically

**Notes:**
- Quantity cannot exceed stock
- Minimum quantity is 1
- Remove item if you decrease to 0

#### Remove Item
1. Click 🗑️ (trash icon) next to item
2. Confirmation dialog appears
3. Click "Remove" to confirm
4. Item removed from cart
5. Cart total recalculates

#### Clear Cart
1. Click "Clear Cart" button (if available)
2. Confirm deletion of all items
3. Cart emptied
4. Return to shopping

## 🎟️ Applying Coupons & Offers

### Finding Active Coupons

**Locations:**
1. **Homepage** - "Active Offers" section
2. **Cart Page** - "Available Offers" section
3. **Checkout Page** - Offer suggestions

### Types of Offers

#### 1. Percentage Discount
- Example: **FIRST20** - 20% off
- **Calculation**: Discount = Subtotal × 20%
- **Max Cap**: May have maximum discount limit

#### 2. Fixed Amount Discount
- Example: **WEEKEND100** - ₹100 off
- **Calculation**: Discount = Fixed ₹100
- **Minimum Order**: Usually required

#### 3. Free Delivery
- Example: **FREEDEL999**
- **Benefit**: Delivery fee waived (saves ₹29)
- **Minimum Order**: ₹999

### Applying a Coupon

**Step-by-Step:**

1. **Copy Coupon Code**
   - Click "Copy Code" on offer card
   - OR manually note the code

2. **Navigate to Cart/Checkout**
   - Open cart page
   - Scroll to coupon section

3. **Enter Code**
```
┌──────────────────────────────┐
│ Have a coupon code?          │
│ ┌──────────────────────┐     │
│ │ FIRST20              │     │
│ └──────────────────────┘     │
│ [Apply Coupon] [Clear]       │
└──────────────────────────────┘
```

4. **Apply & Verify**
   - Click "Apply Coupon"
   - Success message appears
   - Discount reflected in bill summary

### Coupon Validation

**Coupon may fail if:**
- ❌ Code is invalid or expired
- ❌ Minimum order value not met
- ❌ Already used maximum times
- ❌ Not applicable to cart items
- ❌ Another coupon already applied

**Error Messages:**
```
❌ Coupon expired
❌ Minimum order value ₹299 required
❌ This offer is not valid for your cart
❌ Coupon code invalid
```

### Coupon Best Practices

✅ **Check Requirements**
- Minimum order value
- Validity dates
- Product/category restrictions
- Usage limits

✅ **Plan Your Cart**
- Add items to meet minimum
- Remove non-eligible items
- Choose best coupon for your order

✅ **One Coupon Per Order**
- Only one coupon can be applied
- Choose the one with maximum savings
- Compare different coupons

### Coupon Stacking Rules

**Allowed:**
- ✅ Coupon + Free delivery (if eligible by order value)
- ✅ Product discounts + Coupon code

**Not Allowed:**
- ❌ Multiple coupon codes
- ❌ Coupon + another coupon

## ❓ Shopping FAQs

### General Shopping

**Q: Do I need to create an account to browse?**  
A: No, you can browse products without an account. However, you need to login to add items to cart and checkout.

**Q: Can I shop without logging in?**  
A: You can browse but must login to add to cart and place orders.

**Q: How do I know if a product is in stock?**  
A: Stock status is displayed on product cards:
- ✅ "In Stock" - Available
- ⚠️ "Low Stock" - Limited quantity
- ❌ "Out of Stock" - Currently unavailable

### Pricing & Payments

**Q: Are prices inclusive of taxes?**  
A: Yes, all prices displayed include applicable taxes.

**Q: What payment methods are accepted?**  
A: Currently, Cash on Delivery (COD) is available. More payment methods coming soon.

**Q: Can I get a bill/invoice?**  
A: Yes, you'll receive an invoice with your order and via email.

### Discounts & Offers

**Q: Can I use multiple coupons?**  
A: No, only one coupon code can be applied per order.

**Q: Do coupons expire?**  
A: Yes, each coupon has a validity period. Check offer details for expiry date.

**Q: Why is my coupon not working?**  
A: Check:
- Code spelling
- Minimum order value
- Validity dates
- Product eligibility
- One coupon per order rule

### Cart & Wishlist

**Q: How long do items stay in my cart?**  
A: Cart items are saved for 7 days or until you checkout/remove them.

**Q: Can I save my cart for later?**  
A: Yes, items remain in cart between sessions. Use wishlist for long-term saving.

**Q: How do I empty my cart?**  
A: Remove items individually or click "Clear Cart" button.

### Product Availability

**Q: When will out-of-stock items be available?**  
A: Restock dates vary. Add to wishlist to get notified when available.

**Q: Can I pre-order out-of-stock items?**  
A: Currently, pre-orders are not available. Please check back regularly.

**Q: What if my desired quantity is not available?**  
A: You can only add available stock quantity. Check back for restocks.
